## 🧭 On-Page SEO Best Practices

- **Header Hierarchy**: Strictly one `<h1>` per page. Sub-elements are nested progressively: `<h1>` -> `<h2>` -> `<h3>`.
- **Image Accessibility**: Every image includes a descriptive descriptive `alt` attribute. No generic placeholders.
- **Dynamic Meta Tags**:
  ```html
  <title>Lashes & MGlamour | Premium Lash Extensions & Brows Miami</title>
  <meta name="description" content="Discover Miami's premier beauty studio. Professional Classic, Hybrid, and Volume lash extensions, brow shaping, and skin care services. Book your appointment online." />
  <link rel="canonical" href="https://lashesmglamour.com/current-page" />
  ```
- **Social Previews**: Complete OpenGraph (`og:image`, `og:title`, `og:description`) and Twitter Card attributes configured in `src/layouts/Layout.astro`.

## 🌐 Dynamic Assets Configuration

- **`sitemap-index.xml`**: Generated automatically on build by `@astrojs/sitemap` integration, listing all static pages and blog items.

  Allow: /

## 📈 Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Rules

- **The Three-Clicks Rule**: A user arriving at any landing page must be able to book an appointment in less than three clicks:
  1. Click `Book Now` / `FloatingBookButton`.
  2. Select the service category and select the specialist/slot.
  3. Submit name and contact info.
- **Floating Buttons**: Dynamic floats (`FloatingCallButton` and `FloatingBookButton`) appear on mobile screens to ensure direct contact actions are always visible.
